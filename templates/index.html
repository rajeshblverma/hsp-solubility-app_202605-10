Core Framework
The Three Hansen Parameters
A comprehensive framework for understanding molecular interactions through three distinct force components.
δD

Dispersion Forces

London dispersion interactions from temporary dipoles. Present in all molecules — the baseline of molecular cohesion.

δP

Polar Forces

Permanent dipole-dipole interactions. Significant in polar molecules and directly affect solubility in pharmaceutical formulations.

δH

Hydrogen Bonding

Specific hydrogen bond interactions involving hydrogen atoms bonded to electronegative atoms. The strongest of the three parameters.
